Always working for a clean tomorrow

Industrial activities generate considerable amount of waste which is of hazardous/non-hazardous nature due to their characteristics of being corrosive, flammable, reactive, toxic, or persistence in nature. In spite of new technologies, improved production, waste treatment and management strategies like reduce, reuse, and recycle; there is still significant quantity of waste that needs to be treated and disposed in a scientific way.


Utilization of Hazardous Waste as an alternate fuel and raw materials in cement plants for the purpose of energy and resource recovery is known as Pre-Processing. Pre-Processing of hazardous waste is a well established, technically proven and legally approved li solution, promoted and encouraged by MOEF/CPCB and State Pollutions Control Boards (SPC13).
